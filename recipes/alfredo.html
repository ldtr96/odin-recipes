Alfredo

Description

Tender strips of grilled chicken breast atop a bed of pasta sauteed in a rich and creamy Alfredo sauce

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce
